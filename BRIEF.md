# Brief: tilt-crash

**Round:** 16
**Mechanic verb:** SMASH
**One line:** A heavy iron ball on a tilting arena — drag to tip the board, roll into enemies and knock them off the edge; your momentum is your weapon, your downfall, and the whole game.

**Core feel:** Physics weight. Gran Turismo inertia. Tony Hawk momentum. The ball moves like it *costs* something to change direction. Enemies are lighter — they skitter and bounce. You are a wrecking ball.

**Background:** warm amber / terracotta — `#C07838` (not dark)
**BPM:** 118, driving percussive groove

**What makes it this game:** The player has to think ahead — you can't stop on a dime. Tilting to knock one enemy off might send you sliding off the opposite edge two seconds later. The board is the trap for everyone, including you.
